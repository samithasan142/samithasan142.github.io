As a Software QA Engineer at Enosis Solutions with almost 2 years of experience, I am passionate about delivering high-quality software by leveraging my expertise in SDLC, STLC, Agile methodologies, and core SQA principles. My expertise covers Functional, UI, Integration, Black Box, Sanity, Regression, Retesting, and Exploratory testing, as well as API testing using tools like Postman and Insomnia. Actively engaged in Agile processes, I participate in daily SCRUM meetings, sprint planning, and effort estimation. I work closely with developers, QA team members, and project managers to clarify requirements and resolve issues. I am committed to continuous learning and adopting new tools and practices to ensure exceptional product quality.
